Steam Next Fest: June 2024



Steam Next Fest: June 2024 Edition is a multi-day celebration where fans can try out demos, watch livestreams and interact with developers, and learn about upcoming games on Steam. For developers, Steam Next Fest is an opportunity to get early feedback from players and build an audience for a future launch on Steam.

The June 2024 edition runs June 10 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) to June 17 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7).


For general information about Steam Next Fest, including details on registration, preparing your demo, running a livestream, trailer and demo gameplay usage, the Press Preview event, game visibility, and frequently asked questions, please visit the Steam Next Fest parent documentation page.

Eligibility

Steam Next Fest: June 2024 is open to upcoming games that meet all of the following criteria:

  • Associated with a Steamworks developer account in good standing
  • Visible on Steam as Coming Soon at the time of registration 
  • Includes a publicly playable demo by the time the festival begins 
  • To be released after the conclusion of the fest on June 17, 2024

Please Note:
  • All upcoming games may participate with a playable demo in Steam Next Fest, regardless of release date.
  • Choose the Steam Next Fest that works best for your release time. Once your demo participates in an edition, it may not appear in any future Steam Next Fests. The next edition planned is in October 2024.

See your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_jun_2024

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Are the live zoom FAQ sessions recorded?
A. Yes! You can check out our past live Q&A sessions on YouTube here. These sessions cover a wide variety of topics to help you prepare for getting the most out of Steam Next Fest.

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_jun_2024
  • The registration page for your base game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_jun_2024/<appid>
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/<appID>

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Next Fest parent documentation page).

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your base game's appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_jun_2024/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the festival. After opting out, if you have not released your game prior to a future Steam Next Fest, you could participate in that one assuming the rest of the eligibility requirements are met.
