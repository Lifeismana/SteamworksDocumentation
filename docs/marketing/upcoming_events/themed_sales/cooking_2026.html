Steam Cooking Fest 2026



This Fest is for games about cooking! Whether you're running a restaurant, attempting to create dishes together with your friends, or working in the back of a food truck, this Fest is for all delicious games.

This event runs October 12 @ 10:00am PST to October 19 @ 10:00am PST and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.

Eligibility

As you might expect, eligible games will be primarily about cooking or will be culinarily inclined. It is fine to have additional gameplay elements like harvesting ingredients, fighting off bandits, or designing your storefront, but cooking (or serving food) must be an important part of the gameplay.

Realism is not required; though games that carefully mimic real-life cooking are welcome, games that have a more abstract take to cooking are eligible as well.

Hotel/motel simulators, though they occasionally have culinarily minded elements, are not eligible. Restaurant, pub, food truck, cafe, and food factory simulators are eligible as the primary purpose of the building is to serve food. However, games that are mostly about only food delivery may not be eligible. This is Cooking Fest, so cooking is an important element!

Generally speaking, farming simulators and visual novels are not going to be eligible.

Games that include food elements but are not about cooking itself are not eligible. Some examples: counting grains of rice, fighting off bears from stealing your food, and any game where you play food but the goal is not to be cooked. For the sake of this Fest, potions are not cooking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/cooking_2026
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/cooking_2026/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/cooking_2026
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?15216

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/cooking_2026/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
