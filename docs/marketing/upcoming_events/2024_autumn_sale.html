Steam Autumn Sale



The Steam Autumn Sale is all about shopping for great games and nominating your favorites in the 2024 Steam Awards. This year's event runs November 27th - December 4th at 10am Pacific.

Steam Awards Nominations

To solicit your fans' nomination in the Steam Awards, choose the award category for which you'd like to be considered. Use the relevant piece of artwork available via Dropbox here along with your title's box art when communicating with players and fans on Steam and social media. We recommend creating a new "Steam Awards Nomination Suggestion" announcement, which is now available to you from the event dashboard.

For more information about Steam Award nominations and the special event type, visit https://partner.steamgames.com/doc/marketing/event_tools/steamawards.
